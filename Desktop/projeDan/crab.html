INGREDIENTS

1/4 lb crab legs
1/2 cup snow peas
1/2 tsp lemon juice
1 scallion, chopped
1 tsp sake
Salt and sugar to taste
1 tsp tobiko (optional)
Lemon zest, to taste (optional)

GARNISH
Thinly sliced scallions (optional)

Crab and Snow Pea Kanten (agar agar) Squares

This is a perfect appeziter to serve during summer. The chilled kanten (agar agar) is like a chilled soup in solid form. Since kanten has no calories, this is a light and refreshing alternative to the typical party appetizer..

Dissolve one stick of kanten in water for over two hours. Drain, squeezing out all the water. Heat 2 1/4 dashi to boiling, and add the kanten until it melts. Do not sitr until it is dissolved.

Season dashi with salt and sugar to taste. Add sake. Flake crab legs and add to dashi. Chop snow peas on a diagonal and add to dashi. If desired, add finely chopped scallions, lemon zest and tobiko for added flavor and texture. Add lemon juice.

Pour into an 8" x 8" square mold and chill immediately for several hours or overnight.

Dip mold into hot water and turn onto cutting board. Slice into 1.5" squares and serve on a platter or individually on small plates. Garnish with thinly sliced scallions, if desired.

Serves 8-12
